
2 week Minecraft cycle - maze runner

Premise: 

Minecraft maze runner. The base is a small walled off space, and players will explore the outside at night.

[[Design columns]]

**Top down breakdown:**

a small Minecraft world devoid of resources will be walled off from the outside.
The walls will open every day at the same time, and players could get out and try to gather as many resources as possible, as well as explore the outside.
As the night progresses, mob spawns will get more aggressive, making the exploration dangerous and hasty.
At dawn, the doors will close, and players remaining outside will die.


Players will play in loops of chilling/building in the day, then exploring in the night, slowly uncovering the mysteries of the world.

Map layout

Enclosure

The enclosure will be a walled off circle with around 50 blocks radius. The walls will be bedrock and go all the way from the actual bedrock to the build limit.

The floor will be normal, and the way down mostly untouched, with minerals and caves. Just not lava.

The floor will be mostly flat, with nothing on top of it, not even grass.

Underground there will be a few interesting structures

- An old bunker of a past survivor:  
    A small underground cute cubical base containing a note with some clues about how to progress.
    
- Some kind of currency exchange structure
    

  

Outside

### The outside will be divided into 4 sections, walled off from each other with walls dividing out of the enclosure.

Each area will be a different biome, and will contain different resources spread out on the surface. Minerals will be edited to spawn more frequently, so mining under the time pressure will be possible.

The biomes will stretch out for a few hundred blocks, and then be surrounded by an endless sea. 

At least one of the biomes will be surrounded by a sea of lava, making sure players find lava this way and reach the nether.

The nether

The nether will be mostly blank, just endless lava. But inside the enclosure parallel space, there will be some structure that when you interact with it with some item that you find outside, you trigger the final fight to end the game.

If the island at the bottom isnt walled off, players could create a portal to the outside.

They reach the nether quite late into the map, so maybe it's okay to leave out this possibility. It's clever and uses vanilla minecraft features to navigate around the map. 

Maybe just make it a tad bit less effective by adding some challenge when players navigate the outside during the day.

Otherwise if it contradicts some critical design, wall off the nether too.

The end

The portal to the end will be in the center of the map, in the biome that divides the two
