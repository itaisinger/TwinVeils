1. Start    
The players will spawn inside the [[enclosure]], at 6 AM.
The section will be completely flat, grass ground. No trees, maybe just some elevation.
The doors in the walls will be invisible - flushed in with the wall or something. You couldn't make it out there is a wall there.
The walls will be made of bedrock and go all the way up to the build limit.
Like vanilla, a few blocks under the dirt there will be stones and materials, but without wood players will be unable to mine it.
Every third of a day, 4 in-game hours, [[bells]] will ring inside the walls.

2. First night
At 6 PM, the bells will ring 3 times and the walls will open. The outside will reveal a friendly forest area, filled with trees and seeds, but not much else.

Mobs will start spawning soon after.

Throughout the night, the spawns will get more aggressive, or modded mobs will spawn.

When the night is over, if the players are still outside they will generally die, but not instantly. Like in the maze runner, an op monster will spawn. 

Here we will use either the warden or wither, more on that later.

3. The nether
The enclosure will be devoid of lava. Meaning the players will need to find lava elsewhere.

They could find lava either underground outside, which will not be the most practical, or by getting to the edge of one of the biomes, where an endless sea of lava will reside.

And a sign, saying “go to hell”.

The nether will be a completely blank, endless sea of lava.

The only thing in there will be a structure at the center of the place parallel to the enclosure.

In the lava sea there will be an island with a structure missing a central top piece. Underneath there will be a commandblock running a testfor block for that block, so when the players place it there, it will activate the end of the game.

4. Final fight
After placing that block on the nether stracture, a message will appear for all players, “5”. It will be a countdown for a fight taking place inside the enclosure. 

The fight will be hordes of mobs, followed up by a few boss mobs. Or just the warden, looks like its op as hell.

Players will have only one life in this fight, and their objective will be to beat said waves and monsters without being wiped out.

When they do, the map will be over.
