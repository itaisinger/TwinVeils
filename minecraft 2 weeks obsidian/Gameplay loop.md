The main gameplay loop will be centered around day/night.

In the night, players will explore around the outside, trying to gather as many resources and explore as far as they can before time runs out, then try to run back and get into the enclosure safely.

In the day, players will engage in the more typical vanilla minecraft gameplay. They could build their base, farm, mine, craft etc.

As well as vanilla minecraft features, the enclosure will feature a few metroidvenia style un-interactable things. To use them, players will need to first find the appropriate item irinformation outside.
