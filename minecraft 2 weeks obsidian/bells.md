6 times a day, all players will hear a bell ring sound.
at the start of the night and at the start of the day they will hear it 3 times.
in the day/night switch, every ring will be accompanied by a row of bedrock blocks of the [[gates]] appearing or disappearing. 

the bell sounds will work using command blocks, using the command:

`/execute as @a at @s run playsound minecraft:block.bell.hit master @s ~ ~ ~ 1 1`
