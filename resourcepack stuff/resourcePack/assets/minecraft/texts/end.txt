A lone oak lay in a pains glade.
The sun washed its leaves with an eternal afternoon orange. It cast a soft shadow upon the long grass bed. 
That was home.

Warm, familiar, same old home.
Safe, closed. A time capsule.

High, black, titanous walls of bedrock stretch out long farther and higher than what the eye could see. 
The bedrock circled the glade, casting its hard silhouette upon the meadows.
